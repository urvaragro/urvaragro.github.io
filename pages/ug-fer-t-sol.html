Product Description

The SOP is the ideal fermenter for plantes with high nutrient demands, which satisfy the need of the plant, and prevent potential deficiencies, with a high level of Potassium and Sulphur. SOP is nitrogen free, allowing this product to be used according to the needs of many crops, but especially those where nitrogen should be reduced at the fruit maturation stage, maintaining the appropriate N:K ration in this phase. SOP's low salt indices make the product reliable on different types of soils. SOP is the ideal product for fertilisation programmes of cultures grown in high-salinity soils. It is one of the most common sources of hydroponic potassium fertiliser.

Uses

  • Helps plants resist abiotic stress and enhances disease resistance, thereby reducing plant losses
  • For manufacturing: grapes, pomegranate, banana, cotton, tomatoes, onion, ginger, turmeric, hydromelon and protected cultivation.
  • For application in leaflets: All cultivations

Benefit

  • Nitrogen free fertiliser, with high concentration of Potassium and Sulphur.
  • The use of selected SOP in alkaline sols tends to generate a decrease of pH to the root area, which makes phosphorous, and micronutrients more easily assimilated.
  • The SOP selection contains very fine (<0,015 mm) particles which are dissolved quickly when mom-solutions are prepared.
  • Suitable for use when the crop is mature in physiology.
  • Useful for sink filling and correct maturation
  • Increases pest and disease resistance. Helps to manage fungal diseases such as powdery mildew when used for foliar use

Doses

  • 70-100 gm/ 1 litre
  • Use Designate SOP for periods when high calcium Nitrate levels are applied to prevent N imbalance.
  • Avoid using a high level Ca product in water
