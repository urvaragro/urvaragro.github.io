Urvaragro is an agriculture company engaging in research to make agriculture a profitable cooperative engagement for rural societies. The company is leveraging the vast historical data collected for years to analyse various variables involved in farming activity. The study involves parameters about soil health, complex organic and inorganic activities, climatic conditions, biodiversity and other agronomical factors. The company has evolved many statistical models for productive farming from historical data. Every product is an outcome of the holistic research conducted in the field with the goal of sustainable agriculture for humanity.

We are sourcing and producing material worldwide while maintaining our emission target and achieving sustainability goals. Rural livelihood is our primary focus without losing the nutritional value of the food produced. Urvaragro is an agriculture company taking the food security paradigm to nutrition security for humanity.
